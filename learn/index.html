The University of Wisconsin Population Health Institute developed a model of different health measures to demonstrate how different aspects of human society impacts health

About this Visualization

This visualization aims to explain the different health measures in a digestable way

Data Descriptions are supplied by the County Rankings and Roadmaps program at the University of Wisconsin Population Health Institute and pre-processed to be used in this visualization by me.
